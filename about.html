About

On the road since 1997

After Hours Auto is a privately owned Massachusetts carrier with deep roots in the towing and transportation trade. Twenty-eight years in, the model has not changed: our equipment, our drivers, your vehicle handled like it matters.

The short version

A trade learned by doing it

We earned our interstate operating authority in July 1997 and have been hauling ever since. What started in towing and transport work grew into door-to-door enclosed auto and motorcycle shipping across all 48 contiguous states, run today out of Norton, Massachusetts.

The name is not a gimmick. Vehicles break down, deals close, and seasons turn on their own schedule, so we built a company that answers when the office lights are off.

Our drivers are trained, vetted, and experienced with rare and delicate vehicles. They are the backbone of the service, and the reason customers come back.
